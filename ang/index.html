Introduction

Welcome to PolitiScales, the political test that puts you in the political picture. You will be confronted with a series of statements. For each of them, click on the button corresponding to your opinion.


The questions assume that you are a citizen of a nation with a multi-party political system and a market economy. But if this is not the case, this test can still be reliable for a large majority of the answers.

If you don't understand the meaning of a question, try to do some research on the internet to better understand its meaning. Don't put an answer randomly so as not to distort the result.

This test seeks to represent the widest possible range of opinions and therefore contains phrases that may shock you, particularly regarding racism and homophobia.
